# Duality

> **Discover the other side of your story.**

Duality is an AI-powered reflection companion designed to help people develop greater self-awareness through daily and weekly reflection.

Instead of telling users what is true, Duality helps them explore alternative perspectives, identify recurring patterns, challenge assumptions, and reflect more intentionally on their experiences.

The goal is simple:

> Move from _"This is what happened"_ to _"This is one way of seeing what happened."_

---

## Why Duality?

We all tell ourselves stories.

Some empower us. Some limit us. Many go unquestioned.

Duality helps users slow down, reflect, and explore the possibility that there may be another interpretation of their experiences.

By combining structured wellbeing data with free-form journalling, Duality surfaces insights that may otherwise go unnoticed.

---

## Core Philosophy

> Every story has another side.

Duality is built around curiosity rather than certainty.

The AI acts as a reflective companion, helping users explore possibilities instead of providing definitive answers.

It aims to be:

- Curious
- Supportive
- Non-judgemental
- Thought-provoking
- Practical
- Grounded

Duality is **not** therapy, counselling, or a diagnostic tool.

## MVP Features

### Daily Reflection

Users record:

- Sleep hours
- Energy level (1–10)
- Mood level (1–10)
- Stress level (1–10)
- Exercise completed (Yes/No)

They then write or speak about their day.

Duality generates:

- Reflection summary
- Key themes
- Emotional tone
- Possible assumptions
- Alternative perspectives
- One reflection question

### Weekly Reflection

At the end of each week Duality helps users:

- Review recurring themes
- Identify emotional patterns
- Recognise wins
- Highlight growth opportunities
- Surface recurring assumptions
- Generate intentions for the coming week

### Voice Journalling

Users can reflect through:

- Text input
- Voice input (speech-to-text)

Reducing friction makes reflection easier and more natural.

## Example Reflection

### User Entry

> I had a difficult meeting today. It felt like nobody respected my ideas and I left frustrated.

### Duality Response

#### Summary

You felt dismissed during a meeting and left feeling frustrated and unheard.

#### Possible Assumption

You may be assuming that disagreement means your ideas are not valued.

#### Alternative Perspective

Another perspective could be that people were challenging the ideas rather than rejecting you personally.

#### Reflection Question

What evidence supports the idea that your contribution was ignored, and what evidence might suggest otherwise?
